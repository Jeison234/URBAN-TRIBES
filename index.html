URBAN TRIBES IN COLOMBIA

Welcome to the website where we show the different and varied urban tribes found in Colombia, showing us extravagant styles or attitudes.

The most common urban tribes in Colombia include metalheads, punks, rappers, skinheads, rudos, hipsters, among others. Some of these tribes are seen less frequently, while there are others whose popularity is increasing according to technological trends and fashions of the moment.

WHAT'S AN URBAN TRIBE?

The idea of ​​urban tribe is used to name the subcultures that can be found in a city. These are groups of people who dress similarly, have common customs and practices, and share certain thoughts. All these characteristics differentiate members of the urban tribe from citizens in general, who tend to respect the values ​​and traditions of the dominant culture.

types of urban tribes in Colombia:

METALHEADS

The metalheads tribe in Colombia is mainly made up of middle and upper class youth. However, it is not a uniform subculture, rather it groups various styles, beliefs, trends and styles of music. What defines him the most is the taste for metal music, characteristic clothes and some hairstyles.

RAPPERS

They are an urban tribe characterized by listening to rap and hip-hop music. This tribe was born in New York in the 70s and over time it expands. Today we can find rappers all over the world. his ideology focuses on spreading a social message, which criticizes the injustices of the system. In Colombia, rappers can be identified by wearing baggy sweatshirts, plus-size T-shirts (of North American sports teams) and flashy tennis shoes from well-known brands.

PUNKETOS

punketos in Colombia are an urban tribe that share a common taste for punk music. His style is characterized by being disheveled and even careless. They usually wear black clothes and sometimes ripped leather pants or jeans. Ideologically they defend anarchism as an ideology that promotes individual development and the disappearance of the state.

RUDE

The rude boys or rudos, are a group of young people who share a common taste for reggae music. Its ideological principles include rejection by capitalism and the oligarchy. They are also advocates of equality and the union of races.

SKINHEADS

The skinheads or bald men are an urban tribe made up of young people recognized mainly for having their heads shaved. They are a group strongly linked to neo-Nazi movements, since they share a similar ideology of cleansing the species. In that sense, they are an urban tribe with ideas opposed to punks. They can be homophobic, racist, and fascist. However, there are groups of peaceful skin heads who do not display any kind of violent behavior.

HIPSTERS

they are an urban tribe that can be found all over the world. They wear a vintage style, made up of skinny jeans, t-shirts with colorful prints and flashy accessories with a retro flair. Their ideology is to defend nature and take care of the environment. For this reason, they tend to promote environmental causes, consume organic and local products, and their main means of transportation is the bicycle.

MUPPIES

In Colombia, the muppies are an urban tribe that follows trends and dresses in designer clothes. They have an informal style, far from etiquette and conservatism. They usually wear fashionable jean, casual and informal style garments. Muppies use various accessories to decorate their mobile devices. Among his ideology is the responsible consumption of resources, the defense of nature and the pursuit of happiness.

EMOS

His clothing consisted of tight black jean, with a low rise. They wore striped shirts and colored shoelaces. The most distinctive element of the emos was her hair, dropping a thick, straight bangs over one of her eyes. In general, they were seen as a fashion rather than a culture.

SKATERS

Skaters in Colombia are a tribe made up of young people who love extreme sports, particularly skateboarding or skateboarding. They dress comfortably, with skinny pants, baggy T-shirts and flat-soled tennis shoes. They are recognized for having long and disheveled hair.

GEEKS

Geeks in Colombia are characterized by being a group of young people who share an inordinate taste for video games, board games, flashy costumes, and certain movies, comics and television characters.
